Frequently Asked Questions

How does arbitrage trading work?

Arbitrage on Polymarket works by buying both YES and NO tokens when their combined price is less than $1.00. Since one token will always pay out $1.00 at settlement, you're guaranteed a profit equal to $1.00 minus what you paid. It's pure mathematics, not speculation.

Is my money safe?

Yes! We never hold your funds. You maintain complete control of your wallet and private keys. The bot only executes trades on your behalf using signed transactions. You can stop it or withdraw your funds anytime.

How much can I make?

It depends on your capital and market conditions. With $100, expect $10-30/month. With $1,000, expect $100-300/month. Our top users with $5k+ capital are making $500-1,500/month. Results vary, but arbitrage is the safest strategy in trading.

Do I need trading experience?

No! That's the beauty of automation. The bot does everything for you. You just set your budget, click start, and let it run. We provide complete setup guides and 24/7 support if you need help.
